# Shay's Bedroom Environment

## Environment Reference

Shay's bedroom is a specific environment with consistent features that can be referenced for consistent character placement across different "scenes" and scenarios.

---

## Key Features

### Walls
- **Color**: Lilac purple paint
- **Tone**: Soft, warm purple with natural variation (not flat artificial purple)
- **Finish**: Matte or eggshell finish, natural wall texture visible
- **Notes**: The purple provides warm, cozy atmosphere that complements her fair skin tone and creates intimate, personal environment

### Lighting
- **Color Temperature**: 2700K warm white
- **Source**: Warm bedside lamp(s) and/or warm overhead fixture
- **Quality**: Soft, flattering, creates gentle warm glow on skin
- **Effects**: Creates golden/warm skin tones, cozy intimate atmosphere, highlights red/warm accents in outfits

### Decorative Elements
- **Fairy Lights**: String lights (warm white or soft warm tone) creating bokeh background elements
- **Placement**: Along walls, near ceiling, around bed frame, or behind furniture
- **Effect**: Creates soft bokeh points in background, adds depth and cozy atmosphere
- **Appearance**: Visible in background as out-of-focus light points when subject is in foreground

### General Atmosphere
- **Vibe**: Cozy, intimate, personal, youthful
- **Mood**: Warm, inviting, comfortable, lived-in space
- **Color Palette**: Lilac purple (walls) + warm white (lights) = warm purple/warm aesthetic
- **Lighting Feel**: Warm glow, gentle shadows, no harsh contrasts

## Photography Notes

### Lighting Interaction
- Warm 2700K lighting should appear to come from bedside lamp or overhead fixture
- Fairy lights create bokeh when out of focus
- Shadows should be soft, not harsh
- Skin appears with warm golden/pink tones from purple + warm light combo

### Composition Tips
- Use shallow depth of field to keep walls/fairy lights soft
- Subject focus while environment is supporting atmosphere
- Purple walls can provide color contrast or harmony depending on outfit
- Fairy lights add depth and visual interest without distraction

### Camera Settings Simulation
- Aperture: f/1.8 - f/2.8 for bokeh effect
- ISO: 800-1600 for natural low-light feel (if simulating)
- Color temperature: 2700-3000K warm white
- Style: Authentic smartphone or digicam aesthetic
