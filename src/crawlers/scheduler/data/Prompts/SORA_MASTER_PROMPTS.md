## Seated Authority — Vertical Baton

### Complete Prompt

```
Use the woman from the provided reference images as the subject. Her identity must match the reference exactly: same face, bone structure, proportions, skin tone, body shape, and natural features. Do not alter facial geometry, eye spacing, nose shape, jawline, lips, or body proportions. This is a 1:1 identity match to the reference photos.

She is a woman in her mid-20s with long dark brown hair, warm brown-hazel eyes, fair skin with visible natural texture, and defined facial features. No beautification beyond realistic photography.

She is wearing the **Amscan "Stop Traffic" Police Cop Officer costume**:

* Navy blue catsuit with crisp structured collar, cuffed sleeves, center-front zipper with silver badge pull tab, black side stripes along pant legs
* "Stop Traffic" name plate on chest
* Police cap with silver badge
* Faux leather gloves
* Functional belt with adjustable leg-strap holster

Footwear: Classic black Louboutin stiletto heels with signature red sole. Heels are visible in full-body framing, proportionally accurate, and styled as luxury fashion elements rather than costume exaggeration.

Costume should appear authentic and fitted, showing natural body shape with realistic fabric tension and seams.

Pose: Seated on a simple chair or stool, torso upright and composed. One leg crossed over the other at the knee, creating a clean diagonal line. Both hands are holding a police baton vertically, centered between the legs, resting lightly on the floor. The baton is aligned straight and calm, not raised. Expression is neutral and controlled, eyes slightly lowered, head gently tilted forward. Calm authority without aggression.

Framing: Eye-level camera angle. Camera positioned directly in front with no tilt. Full-body editorial framing with slight asymmetry, subject positioned slightly right of center. Clean negative space around the subject. Chair legs and floor are visible. Natural proportions, no wide-angle distortion.

Footwear emphasis: Louboutin high heels are clearly visible, one heel grounded, the other subtly elevated due to the leg cross, with the red sole faintly visible.

Prop interaction: Baton is held casually and confidently between legs, never obstructing face or body silhouette. No restraint, no implied action — purely visual styling consistent with fashion editorial photography.

Photography Style: High-end fashion catalog photography. Realistic skin texture, visible pores, natural complexion. Raw, authentic photo style — not airbrushed, not stylized. True-to-life proportions and anatomy.

Technical Capture: Simulated high-definition digital capture using a Phase One medium-format camera. 50-70mm lens equivalent. Crystal-clear sharpness, no motion blur, no noise. Ultra-high resolution, 8K quality.

Lighting & Background: Soft, even studio lighting with no harsh shadows. Light coming slightly from one side, but still flat enough for catalog realism. Clean, minimal interior or studio environment. Background elements are soft and non-distracting.

Tone: confident, stylish, editorial, fashion-forward, non-provocative.
```

## 3 Seated Variations (Same Framing)

1. **Vertical Baton** (Hero)
   - Baton centered between crossed legs
   - Eyes slightly lowered, neutral authority

2. **Horizontal Baton** (Relaxed)
   - Baton held across lap horizontally
   - Direct eye contact, slight smile

3. **No Baton** (Classic)
   - Hands resting on knees
   - Classic seated portrait feel

All maintain: upright torso, eye-level camera, slight asymmetry, editorial framing.

---

## Technical Notes

* The vertical baton creates strong composition line through frame
* Crossed legs add visual rhythm and diagonal energy
* Asymmetrical framing (subject slightly right) feels more editorial than centered
* Eye-level angle avoids power dynamics of looking down or up
* Calm expression reads as authority, not aggression
* Red sole visible due to leg cross angle
* Clean background keeps focus on subject and pose
