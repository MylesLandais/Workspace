## Seated Authority Poses

## seated_chair_vertical_baton
- **Short**: "seated with vertical baton"
- **Description**: seated on simple chair or stool, torso upright and composed, one leg crossed over other at the knee, both hands holding police baton vertically centered between legs, baton resting lightly on floor, aligned straight and calm. Expression neutral and controlled, eyes slightly lowered, head gently tilted forward, calm authority without aggression
- **Camera Notes**: eye-level or very slightly below eye level, camera positioned directly in front with no tilt, full-body editorial framing with slight asymmetry, subject positioned slightly right of center, clean negative space around subject, chair legs and floor visible, natural proportions, no wide-angle distortion
