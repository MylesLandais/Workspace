## Whistle Actions

### whistle_held_loosely_chin
- **Short**: "whistle held loosely against chin"
- **Description**: Silver referee whistle held casually and loosely against or near the chin, not ready to blow, relaxed hand position, soft and intimate vibe
- **Camera Notes**: close framing shows hand relaxed, whistle visible but not threatening

### whistle_pressed_bottom_lip
- **Short**: "whistle pressed against bottom lip playfully"
- **Description**: Silver referee whistle pressed gently against the bottom lip, playful positioning, one second away from blowing, creates tension and anticipation
- **Camera Notes**: extreme close-up captures whistle detail and lip expression

### whistle_poised_ready_blow
- **Short**: "whistle poised ready to blow"
- **Description**: Silver whistle positioned directly against lips, ready to blow at any second, subject taking sharp inhale with chest slightly expanded, tense anticipation
- **Camera Notes**: capture the split second before action, tension in face and body

### whistle_twirling_loose
- **Short**: "whistle twirling loosely"
- **Description**: Whistle twirled loosely on its cord or held spinning casually in one hand, relaxed playful energy, prop used as styling element not performance
- **Camera Notes**: hand positioned to show twirling motion or relaxed hold

### whistle_against_cheek
- **Short**: "whistle pressed against cheek"
- **Description**: Silver whistle pressed lightly against cheek or held near face, playful and intimate positioning, not ready to blow
- **Camera Notes**: side angle or profile shows whistle placement well

### whistle_between_lips_horizontal
