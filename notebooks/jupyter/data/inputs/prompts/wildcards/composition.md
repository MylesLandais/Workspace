# Composition

## Shot Types / Scale

### head_shoulders
- **Short**: "head and shoulders"
- **Description**: head and shoulders composition, face occupies 40-60% of frame, tight crop showing head and upper shoulders, portrait focus
- **Camera Notes**: standard portrait framing, face is primary focus, minimal body context

### medium_shot
- **Short**: "medium shot"
- **Description**: medium composition from head to mid-torso, shows shoulders, neck, and partial torso, provides spatial context
- **Camera Notes**: wider framing, shows body posture and size relationships

### full_body_composition
- **Short**: "full body composition"
- **Description**: complete full-body shot, subject fully visible from head to toe, balanced vertical composition
- **Camera Notes**: classic fashion catalog framing, shows entire outfit and stance

### three_quarter_body
- **Short**: "three-quarter body"
- **Description**: body shot from head to knees, partial legs visible, balanced medium composition between headshot and full-body
- **Camera Notes**: fashion photography standard, shows upper body composition and partial legwear

## Camera Angles & Perspective

### side_profile
- **Short**: "side profile"
- **Description**: complete side profile view, subject facing 90° away from camera, full side silhouette, strong linear composition
- **Camera Notes**: 90 degree angle, full side view, emphasizes profile features and outline

### back_view
- **Short**: "back view"
- **Description**: camera positioned directly behind subject showing full back of head, neck, and shoulders, emphasizes hairstyle and back composition
- **Camera Notes**: pure back view, no face visible, best for hairstyle and back detail

### three_quarter_back
- **Short**: "three-quarter back view"
- **Description**: photographed from three-quarter back view, head turned slightly away from camera showing profile, layered depth with back and profile visible
- **Camera Notes**: 120-135 degree angle from frontal, shows both face profile and back hairstyle

### over_shoulder
- **Short**: "over-the-shoulder look back"
- **Description**: looking back over shoulder toward camera with soft genuine smile, photographed from rear three-quarter angle, dynamic turn creating engagement
- **Camera Notes**: rear three-quarter angle, subject looking back at camera, engaging composition

### elevated_rear
- **Short**: "elevated rear view"
- **Description**: camera positioned above and behind showing clear top-down view of head and shoulders, high-angle composition emphasizing hair from above
- **Camera Notes**: elevated angle looking down, shows top of head and hairstyle structure

## Balance & Symmetry

### centered_subject
- **Short**: "centered subject composition"
- **Description**: subject positioned dead center of frame, symmetrical balanced composition, classic centered portrait
- **Camera Notes**: strong central focus, balanced negative space on all sides

### off_center_rule_of_thirds
- **Short**: "off-center, rule of thirds"
- **Description**: subject positioned off-center using rule of thirds grid, dynamic asymmetrical balance, more engaging than centered
- **Camera Notes**: subject at 1/3 or 2/3 horizontal position, dynamic visual tension

### negative_space_left
- **Short**: "negative space on left"
- **Description**: subject positioned to the right, negative space on left side of frame, breathing room, editorial composition
- **Camera Notes**: text or graphics could be placed on left side, dynamic balance

### negative_space_right
- **Short**: "negative space on right"
- **Description**: subject positioned to the left, negative space on right side of frame, breathing room, editorial composition
- **Camera Notes**: text or graphics could be placed on right side, dynamic balance

### symmetrical_balance
- **Short**: "symmetrical composition"
- **Description**: perfectly symmetrical arrangement, mirrored elements on left and right, formal balanced composition
- **Camera Notes**: classic formal portrait, stable composition

### asymmetrical_tension
- **Short**: "asymmetrical composition with tension"
- **Description**: off-center positioning creates visual tension, dynamic arrangement, eye moves through frame
- **Camera Notes**: engaging composition, less predictable than centered

## Dynamic vs Static

### dynamic_diagonal
- **Short**: "dynamic diagonal composition"

- **Camera Notes**: angled composition, active and energetic

### motion_blur_dynamic
- **Short**: "motion blur dynamic"

### vertical_emphasis
- **Short**: "vertical emphasis"

- **Camera Notes**: portrait orientation, vertical flow and stability

### horizontal_flow
- **Short**: "horizontal flow"
