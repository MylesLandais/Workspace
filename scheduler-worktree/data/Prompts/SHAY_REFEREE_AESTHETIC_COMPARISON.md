# Shay Racy Referee - Aesthetic Comparison

## Overview

Two complete test prompt sets for Shay's Racy Referee costume:
1. **2009 MySpace/Emo Vibe** - Grainy, moody, raw authentic mobile photography
2. **Polished Girlfriend Message** - Smooth, flawless, clean Snapchat-style photos

---

## Aesthetic Differences

| Aspect | 2009 Emo/MySpace | Polished Girlfriend |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Skin Appearance** | Raw, natural texture, visible grain | Smooth, flawless, healthy glow |
| **Lighting** | Dim, moody, harsh flash, blue TV glow | Bright, warm, flattering, even illumination |
| **Editing** | No processing, raw authenticity | Light touchup, smoothing filter, enhanced |
| **Composition** | Overhead angles, tilted, imperfect | Perfectly centered, well-composed, intentional |
| **Vibe** | Misunderstood angst, rebellion, sad | Warm affection, loving, excited, supportive |
| **Message** | Late-night emo feeling | "Good morning," "thinking of you," "miss you" |

## Usage Guidelines

### Choose 2009 Emo/MySpace When:
- Wanting nostalgic, vintage mobile photography feel
- Targeting misunderstood youth angst aesthetic
- Needing moody, dramatic, emotional vibe
- Going for raw authenticity over polished perfection

### Choose Polished Girlfriend When:
- Wanting modern, clean, aesthetic photos
- Targeting loving, affectionate girlfriend energy
- Needing flawless skin appearance with glow/touchup
- Going for intentional curated "message to boyfriend" vibe

### Shared Elements Across Both:
- Shay's Racy Referee costume (identical wardrobe)
- Shay's bedroom environment (lilac walls, fairy lights)
- Identity lock (100% match to reference)
- Whistle actions and interactions
- Smartphone/Snapchat authentic framing
