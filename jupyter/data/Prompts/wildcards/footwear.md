## Louboutin Styling Notes

**Full-body framing**: Red sole should be visible to capture brand identity
**Knee position**: Slight knee bend creates natural, elegant line
**Foot position**: One foot subtly forward adds dynamism
**Camera angle**: Eye-level or slightly lower angle emphasizes heel length
**Proportion**: Heels styled as luxury fashion elements, not costume exaggeration
