# Poses

## neutral_frontal
- **Short**: "neutral expression, frontal"
- **Description**: facing camera directly with a neutral, relaxed expression, eyes looking at camera
- **Camera Notes**: frontal angle, head-on view

## genuine_smile_frontal
- **Short**: "genuine smile, frontal"
- **Description**: facing camera, genuine smile showing upper teeth, eyes slightly squinted, warm friendly expression
- **Camera Notes**: frontal angle, head-on view

## big_laugh
- **Short**: "big laugh"
- **Description**: mid-laugh expression with mouth open, head tilted back slightly, joyful expression
- **Camera Notes**: slight up angle shows head tilt

## serious_focused
- **Short**: "serious focused expression"
- **Description**: neutral to slightly serious expression, eyes focused just past the camera, contemplative look
- **Camera Notes**: frontal or slight angle

## thoughtful_looking_down
- **Short**: "thoughtful, looking down"
- **Description**: looking slightly down and to the side, thoughtful expression, contemplative mood
- **Camera Notes**: slight down angle, shows downward gaze

## surprised_intrigued
- **Short**: "surprised intrigued"
- **Description**: mildly surprised expression with raised eyebrows and parted lips, engaged look
- **Camera Notes**: frontal angle best

## gentle_smile_closed_mouth
- **Short**: "gentle smile, closed mouth"
- **Description**: gentle closed-mouth smile, soft friendly expression
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable

## tired_contemplative
- **Short**: "tired contemplative"
- **Description**: slight under-eye shadows, contemplative expression, relaxed tired look
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable, indoor settings

## confident_smirk
- **Short**: "confident smirk"
- **Description**: subtle smirk, chin slightly raised, confident expression
- **Camera Notes**: slight up angle shows raised chin

## slight_3_4_right
- **Short**: "slight 3/4 right"
- **Description**: head turned slightly 3/4 to the right, eyes looking towards camera, neutral expression
- **Camera Notes**: 30-45 degree angle from frontal

## slight_3_4_left
- **Short**: "slight 3/4 left"
- **Description**: head turned slightly 3/4 to the left, eyes looking towards camera, neutral to slight smile
- **Camera Notes**: 30-45 degree angle from frontal

## right_3_4_profile
- **Short**: "right 3/4 profile"
- **Description**: 3/4 profile from the right side, eyes looking forward past the camera, neutral expression
- **Camera Notes**: 45-60 degree angle, shows side of face

## left_3_4_profile
- **Short**: "left 3/4 profile"
- **Description**: 3/4 profile from the left side, neutral to slight smile, side view
- **Camera Notes**: 45-60 degree angle, shows side of face

## near_profile_side
- **Short**: "near profile side view"
- **Description**: close to full side profile, ear visible, neutral lips, side silhouette
- **Camera Notes**: 75-90 degree angle, full side view

## slight_up_angle
- **Short**: "slight up angle"
- **Description**: camera placed slightly below eye level, subject looking just past camera with relaxed expression
- **Camera Notes**: low camera angle, looking up slightly

## slight_down_angle
- **Short**: "slight down angle"
- **Description**: camera slightly above eye level, subject looking up towards camera, friendly expression
- **Camera Notes**: high camera angle, looking up

## head_tilt_right
- **Short**: "head tilt right"
- **Description**: head tilted gently to the right, soft smile, natural pose
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable, shows tilt

## head_tilt_left
- **Short**: "head tilt left"
- **Description**: head tilted gently to the left, neutral expression, natural pose
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable, shows tilt

## looking_away
- **Short**: "looking away"
- **Description**: 3/4 view, looking out of frame towards a window or distance, thoughtful expression
- **Camera Notes**: 3/4 angle, gaze away from camera
