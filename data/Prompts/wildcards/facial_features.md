# Facial Features

## balanced_oval_face
- **Short**: "balanced oval face"
- **Description**: balanced oval-to-heart face shape, softly pronounced cheekbones, feminine and delicate jawline, slightly tapered natural chin

## refined_symmetry
- **Short**: "refined facial symmetry"
- **Description**: refined symmetry and elegant proportions, straight to softly contoured nose bridge, balanced facial thirds

## eyes_soft_gaze
- **Short**: "soft eyes"
- **Description**: clear almond-to-rounded eyes with a soft gaze, natural lash line

## editorial_barefaced
- **Short**: "editorial barefaced"
- **Description**: front-facing barefaced portrait, natural skin texture with neutral-to-cool undertones, no makeup, hair pulled back, calm neutral expression, faint blue outline tracing face shape

## editorial_makeup
- **Short**: "editorial makeup"
- **Description**: fresh luminous skin with a natural satin finish, modern European soft glam makeup, gentle blush tones, nude pink or soft rose glossy lips, subtle eyeliner with softly lifted outer corners, natural lashes
