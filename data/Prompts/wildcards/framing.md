# Framing

## head_shoulders
- **Short**: "head and shoulders"
- **Description**: head and shoulders framing, face occupies 40-60% of frame, tight crop showing head and upper shoulders
- **Camera Notes**: standard portrait framing, face is primary focus

## medium_shot
- **Short**: "medium shot"
- **Description**: medium shot from head to mid-torso, shows shoulders, neck, and partial torso, provides spatial context
- **Camera Notes**: wider framing, shows body posture and size relationships

## back_view
- **Short**: "back view"
- **Description**: camera positioned directly behind subject showing full back of head, neck, and shoulders, emphasizes hairstyle
- **Camera Notes**: pure back view, no face visible, best for hairstyle detail

## three_quarter_back
- **Short**: "three-quarter back view"
- **Description**: photographed from three-quarter back view, head turned slightly away from camera showing profile, emphasizes back-of-head hairstyle while showing side of face
- **Camera Notes**: 120-135 degree angle from frontal, shows both face profile and back hairstyle

## over_shoulder
- **Short**: "over-the-shoulder look back"
- **Description**: looking back over shoulder toward camera with soft genuine smile, photographed from rear three-quarter angle, shows both face and back hairstyle
- **Camera Notes**: rear three-quarter angle, subject looking back at camera

## side_profile
- **Short**: "side profile"
- **Description**: complete side profile view, woman facing 90° away from camera, shows full side silhouette
- **Camera Notes**: 90 degree angle, full side view, emphasizes profile features

## elevated_rear
- **Short**: "elevated rear view"
- **Description**: camera positioned above and behind showing clear top-down view of head and shoulders, emphasizes hairstyle from above
- **Camera Notes**: elevated angle looking down, shows top of head and hairstyle structure
