# Hairstyles

## down
- **Short**: "down hair, center part"
- **Description**: long dark brown hair worn completely down, center-parted, flowing freely past shoulders to mid-chest length, straight with slight natural wave/body
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable

## ponytail
- **Short**: "ponytail"
- **Description**: long dark brown hair gathered into a ponytail, center part maintained at front, hair extending to mid-chest when down
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable, back view shows ponytail structure

## claw_clip_half_up
- **Short**: "claw clip half-up"
- **Description**: half-up style with oversized black claw clip positioned horizontally at upper crown area, approximately 4-5 inches from hairline. Upper section of thick hair gathered smoothly into clip creating subtle volume lift at crown, lower section flowing freely down back to mid-chest length. Face-framing pieces left loose or pulled back smoothly. Casual, practical, everyday wear styling.
- **Camera Notes**: three-quarter back view recommended for clip visibility, back view shows clip placement and hair flow

## claw_clip_full_updo
- **Short**: "full updo claw clip"
- **Description**: complete full updo with ALL long dark brown hair gathered upward from nape of neck, sides, and crown, pulled upward and secured at upper back/crown area with oversized pearl-decorated black claw clip positioned horizontally. Entire hair length twisted/bundled and clipped, creating elevated gathered bun silhouette. Clean exposed neckline, sleek pulled-back front, possible subtle face-framing wisps or tendrils. Hair appears polished yet effortlessly casual.
- **Camera Notes**: back view or three-quarter back required to show updo structure, clip placement, and exposed neckline. Pure back view shows maximum updo emphasis. Over-the-shoulder look back shows face + updo.

## styled_curled
- **Short**: "styled curled hair"
- **Description**: long dark brown hair styled with curls or waves, center part maintained, hair extending to mid-chest length, styled appearance with body and texture
- **Camera Notes**: any angle suitable, shows styled texture and movement
